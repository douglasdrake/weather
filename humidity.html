Humidity

Humidity ranges from 60% to 100% in a narrow window (-5, 5) near the equator. Outside of this window, we see much more variation in humidity percents. In the northern hemisphere, humidities range from just above 0% to 100%. In the southern hemisphere, humidities range from 20% to 100%.

We observe that there are two areas of high density in the joint distribution of latitude and humidity with humidity approximately 80% and latitudes near 0 (the equator) and 60.
